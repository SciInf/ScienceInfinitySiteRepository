2013 Inland Regional Science Bowl: Press Release 3/2/2013

The Science Infinity is formed for the following purposes

  • Prepare students for academic excellence in science through coaching, on-the-go interactions and competitions with other like-minded students
  • Develop young leaders by providing leadership roles and opportunities to achieve the current mission.
  • Raise the bar in science education and research.

This is a non profit organization. We share simillar goals as North South Foundation (NSF). Since it is run exclusively by volunteers, we don't have expenses. The only expenses we incur is if we buy any student supplies.
